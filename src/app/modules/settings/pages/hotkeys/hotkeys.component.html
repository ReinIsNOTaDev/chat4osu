Hotkeys

App hotkeys

Note: These hotkeys cannot be changed for now.

  • Ctrl+N
    Join channel
  • Ctrl+W
    Leave channel
  • Ctrl+→ / Ctrl+Tab
    Cycle to next channel
  • Ctrl+← / Ctrl+Shift+Tab
    Cycle to previous channel
  • Ctrl+1 - 8
    Cycle to a specific tab (1-8)
  • Ctrl+9
    Cycle to the last tab
